ABOUT

Re:Play is a story about Toshiro who "accidentaly" enters a mobile game he got from an illegal website. As soon as he enters this new realm, he's wanted for murder, theft and many other accusations. As he unfolds what's going on with time, he realizes he's not in the amazing worlds people enter in the isekai books he reads He meets his so-called "partner in crime" Mayu Saralira, a well known assasin who trains Toshiro into his final form.
